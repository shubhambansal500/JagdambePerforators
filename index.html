Our Story

Who We Are

Jagdambe Perforators is one of India's trusted manufacturers of precision perforated metal sheets and cable trays — built on a foundation of quality, innovation, and customer trust.

Established over two decades ago, we have grown into a full-scale manufacturing unit equipped with modern CNC presses and automated perforating lines. Our facility produces a wide variety of hole patterns in mild steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and galvanised sheets — tailored to exact customer specifications.

We supply to OEMs, project contractors, architects, and industrial buyers across India. Our stringent quality checks and on-time delivery track record have earned us long-term partnerships with clients in some of the most demanding industries.
